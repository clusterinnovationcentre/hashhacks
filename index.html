  • FAQ

    Can I attend?

    IF (you are a college student) AND (passionate about Computer Science) AND (you are at least 18 years old)) RETURN "Yes!"

    What are the prizes?

    Prizes worth LAKHS that include CASH, Wolfram API, JetBrains Licences, Microsoft Azure Credits, stickers, swag and other in-kind goodies from our partners. We have something for each and every attendee and obviously special prizes for finalists!

    How does registration work?

    Registration is open until October 16th, 11:59 pm IST! Applicants will be selected on the basis of Github & LinkedIn profile, resume, past projects and the answers to questions given in the registration form. If you’re accepted, you’ll have two days to confirm your attendance.

    Does this cost money?

    No! The event is free to attend. We have free snacks, but three full course meals are available for 300, or you can avail them individually at 100 per meal! We know, everyone expects the food to be free, but we assure you, the hackathon is fun! P.S. It has been for the past 3 years!

    What will I eat?

    We've got free snacks! Coffee will be there too of! Of course, the college cafetaria will be open throughout the hacking period and you may also order from restaurants that are open all night in the campus area through Zomato. We'll also have arrangements for Monster energy drinks. You can avail the full course meals at 100 per meal, or submit 300 combined to the organizing team.

    How do I get there?

    The event venue is: Cluster Innovation Centre, Rugby Sevens Building, University Stadium, University of Delhi, G.C Narang Marg, Delhi, 110007. This is at a 5 minutes walking distance from Vishwavidyalaya Delhi Metro Station (Yellow Line). Approach towards gate number 2 of stadium. Please avoid bringing your own vehicles as the parking space (available at gate number 2c) is limited!

    What should I bring?

    Bring a valid student ID for admission and a laptop for hacking. Also bring either a soft or hard copy of the event pass mailed to you if after confirming your participation. We'll be needing the QR code to check you in.

    How do teams work?

    Teams are at most 4 people. You can select teammates when you register, or you can register individually without specified teammates. We’ll select candidates by teams, so rest assured you’ll be able to hack with your friends if that’s what you prefer!

    What about hardware hacks?

    You are allowed to bring your own hardware if you like. We'll provide with you an amazing internet connection and good comfortable working space to build your dream product.

    What’s the schedule?

    Check-in starts at 14:30 on 19th Oct followed by introduction at 16:30. Coding period begins at 17:00 and continues till 13:00 the next day. Final judging process will begin at 15:30 and distribution of prizes at 17:00.

    What if this FAQ didn’t answer my questions?

    Please email us at convoke4.0@gmail.com if you’re in any way confused or concerned! We’d love to help you out. You may also reach out to us on Facebook at www.facebook.com/convoke.ducic or whatsapp us at 9910268058 or call at 7678640404:)

  • Rules, T&C

    Rules

    Teams can hack in any of the following themes Artificial Intelligence, IOT, Algorithms, FinTech, Blockchain, Machine Learning. Participants must code overnight to develop a product around these themes. Work must be original. Plagiarism will lead to immediate disqualification. Any illegal activity (drinking, smoking etc. within campus) is prohibited and will be dealt with seriously.

    Evaluation

    Through continuous evaluation, mentorship and feedback mechanism; we aim to ensure that thorough analysis is performed and teams are judged accordingly. We will also try to automate some of the judging processes such as code quality through Github bots and ensure smooth running of the hackathon via bots on other platforms as well. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
