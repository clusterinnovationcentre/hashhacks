  • FAQ

    Can I attend?

    IF (you are a college undergraduate) AND (passionate about Computer Science) AND (you are at least 18 years old)) RETURN "Yes!"

    What are the prizes?

    Prizes worth LAKHS that include CASH, Wolfram API, JetBrains Licences, Digital Ocean coupons, stickers, swag and other in-kind goodies from our partners. We have something for each and every attendee and obviously special prizes for finalists!

    How does registration work?

    Registration is open until October 20th, 11:59 pm IST! Applicants will be selected on the basis of Github & LinkedIn profile, resume, past projects and the answers to questions given in the registration form. If you’re accepted, you’ll have two days to confirm your attendance.

    Does this cost money?

    A registration fees of Rs. 300 will have to be paid online to confirm attendance once the applicant is selected. This includes meals, drinks, snacks, overnight hosting, mentorship, swag, awesome prizes and a memorable experience (if we do say so ourselves)! Just ask your friends/seniors who attended last time if you know any. Honestly, the appreciation we solicited last time is what propels us to continue hosting awesome events.

    What will I eat?

    We’ll provide food for dinner on Friday and breakfast, lunch, snacks on Saturday. Coffee will be there too! Of course, the college cafetaria will be open throughout the hacking period and you may also order from restaurants that are open all night in the campus area through Zomato. We'll also have arrangements for RedBull energy drinks.

    How do I get there?

    The event venue is: Cluster Innovation Centre, Rugby Sevens Building, University Stadium, University of Delhi, G.C Narang Marg, Delhi, 110007. This is at a 5 minutes walking distance from Vishwavidyalaya Delhi Metro Station (Yellow Line). Approach towards gate number 2 of stadium. Please avoid bringing your own vehicles as the parking space (available at gate number 2c) is limited!

    What should I bring?

    Bring a valid student ID for admission and a laptop for hacking. Also bring either a soft or hard copy of the event pass mailed to you if after confirming your participation. We'll be needing the QR code to check you in.

    How do teams work?

    Teams are at most 4 people. You can select teammates when you register, or you can register individually without specified teammates. We’ll select candidates by teams, so rest assured you’ll be able to hack with your friends if that’s what you prefer!

    What about hardware hacks?

    You are allowed to bring your own hardware if you like. We'll provide with you an amazing internet connection and good comfortable working space to build your dream product.

    What’s the schedule?

    Check-in starts at 14:30 on 26th Oct followed by introduction at 15:30. Coding period begins at 16:00 and continues till 15:00 the next day. Final judging process will begin at 15:30 and distribution of prizes at 17:00.

    What if this FAQ didn’t answer my questions?

    Please email us at hashinclude@ducic.ac.in if you’re in any way confused or concerned! We’d love to help you out. You may also reach out to us on Facebook at www.facebook.com/convoke.ducic or whatsapp/call us at 9910268058. :)

  • Rules, T&C

    Rules

    Teams can hack in any of the following themes Artificial Intelligence, IOT, Algorithms, FinTech, Blockchain, Machine Learning. Participants must code overnight to develop a product around these themes. Work must be original. Plagiarism will lead to immediate disqualification. Any illegal activity (drinking, smoking etc. within campus) is prohibited and will be dealt with seriously.

    Schedule

    26th October
    13:30 Check-In
    14:00 to 15:00 Informing participants and Disclosure of themes
    15:00 Coding Starts
    17:05 to 19:00 Ideation Phase
    19:00 First evaluation/discussion while coding continues
    20:00 Dinner
    27th October
    02:00 Second evaluation
    08:00 Breakfast
    10:00 Third evaluation
    13:00 Lunch
    14:00 Elevator Pitch
    15:00 Shortlisted teams announced
    15:30 Presentation in front of judges
    17:00 Prize and Certificate distribution

    Evaluation

    Through continuous evaluation, mentorship and feedback mechanism; we aim to ensure that thorough analysis is performed and teams are judged accordingly. We will also try to automate some of the judging processes such as code quality through Github bots and ensure smooth running of the hackathon via bots on other platforms as well. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
